## Project Overview

This project delivers an end-to-end operational business intelligence dashboard analyzing **1,000 localized ride-sharing bookings** across the Delhi NCR marketplace. The objective is to identify systemic revenue leakage points, evaluate fleet utilization thresholds across 6 vehicle tiers, and diagnose behavioral root causes for marketplace cancellations.

Engineered as a core portfolio component to demonstrate professional-grade competence in **dimensional data modeling, advanced DAX pattern synthesis, and executive UI/UX dashboard layout architecture**.

## Analytics Dashboard Framework

The interactive workspace is partitioned into a 4-page executive interface mapped directly to business operations:

### 1. Booking Status & Fulfillment Pipeline
- **Operational Fulfillment:** Evaluates the conversion baseline from request to completion across 1,000 transactions.
- **Baseline Stabilization:** Successful completion rate settles at a **63.3% threshold** (633 fulfilled rides).
- **Temporal Volume Tracking:** Tracks monthly request trends to identify marketplace supply-demand imbalances.

### 2. Cancellation Mechanics & Root-Cause Analysis
- **Leakage Isolation:** Diagnoses customer-initiated cancellations (**17.6%**) vs. driver-initiated cancellations (**5.9%**) to establish operational accountability.
- **Fulfillment Deficits:** Pinpoints the **6.8% "No Driver Found" rate** to expose regional supply density deficits.
- **Behavioral Categorization:** Aggregates unstructured cancellation arguments (e.g., driver delays, vehicle limits) into actionable workflow friction points.

### 3. Fleet Segmentation & Performance Dynamics
- **Fleet Tier Profiling:** Evaluates performance matrices across 6 distinct vehicle classes (*Go Mini, Go Sedan, Auto, eBike/Bike, UberXL, Premier Sedan*).
- **Logistics Intersections:** Cross-references average Vehicle Travel Arrival Times (VTAT) and Customer Travel Arrival Times (CTAT) against vehicle utilization volume.
- **Value Distribution:** Verifies pricing structures through distance-to-booking correlation heatmaps.

### 4. Transactional Revenue & Liquidity Distribution
- **Capital Flow Settlement:** Maps top-line revenue distribution across 5 primary payment mechanisms (*UPI, Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card, Uber Wallet*).
- **Channel Dominance:** Identifies **UPI** as the primary liquid transaction channel.
- **Concentration Analysis:** Leverages customer filtering logic to isolate high-value user cohorts and top spending segments.

## Executive Insights Summary

1. **Fulfillment Bottlenecks Stems from Customer Attrition:** The primary threat to marketplace fulfillment is customer-side cancellations (17.6%), which outpace active driver rejections (5.9%) by nearly 3x. Minimizing wait-time velocity (VTAT) is critical to curbing customer attrition.
2. **Supply Density Gaps:** A clear 6.8% of marketplace requests failure is due to "No Driver Found". This points to localized micro-supply deficits that require predictive, time-bound driver staging incentives.
3. **Digital Transaction Ecosystem Dominance:** UPI acts as the primary transaction clearing network for top-line revenue. Integrating seamless, low-latency UPI payment validations directly optimizes post-ride marketplace checkout speeds.
4. **Structured Pricing Integrity:** Cross-axis visualizations confirm a consistent, direct correlation between trip distance and transaction final value, verifying systemic billing execution accuracy across all 6 vehicle tiers.
